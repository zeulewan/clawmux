Privacy Policy

ClawMux does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

What the app does

  • Runs entirely on your local machine — your voice, your code, your data stays on your hardware
  • Speech-to-text and text-to-speech are processed locally via Whisper and Kokoro
  • No audio is sent to external services

Data collection

  • There is no analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
  • No usage data or crash reports are collected
  • No cookies, fingerprinting, or third-party scripts

Network access

  • ClawMux communicates only between your local machine and your Claude Code sessions
  • The hub server binds to your local network — no data is routed through external servers

Contact

If you have questions, open an issue at github.com/zeulewan/clawmux/issues
